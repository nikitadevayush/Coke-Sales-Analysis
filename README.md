# Coke-Sales-Analysis
1️⃣ Understanding the Problem Statement

In a competitive U.S. retail beverage market, Coca-Cola must balance:

* Revenue growth

* Operating profitability

* Retailer partnerships

* Regional performance

* Seasonal demand fluctuations

The goal of this analysis is to use the provided Excel sales data to identify:

* High-performing retailers

* Profitable regions

* Strong and weak beverage brands

* Time-based sales patterns and translate these findings into actionable business recommendations.

2️⃣ What Data Exists?

A. Data Source: Excel dataset

B. Granularity: Sales data at retailer, region, brand, and invoice-date level

C. Key Columns

* Retailer

* Region

* Invoice Date

* Beverage Brand

* Units Sold

* Price per Unit

* Total Sales

* Operating Profit

* Operating Margin (%)

D. Data Preparation

* Standardized column headers

* Formatted dates and numeric fields

* Verified calculations for:

  a. Total Sales

  b. Operating Profit

  c. Operating Margin

* Prepared pivot-ready tables for aggregation

3️⃣ Key Metrics & KPIs Used

A. Core Metrics

* Total Sales → Revenue performance

* Units Sold → Demand indicator

* Operating Profit → Absolute profitability

* Operating Margin (%) → Cost efficiency

B. Analytical Breakdowns

* Sales by Retailer

* Profitability by Region

* Monthly sales trends

* Price vs volume relationship

4️⃣ Analysis & Findings

A. Retailer Performance Analysis

Retailer-wise aggregation of Total Sales shows:

* Sodapop is the top-performing retailer

* Total Sales from Sodapop: $4,403,630

Insight:
This indicates strong distribution reach, shelf presence, and retailer partnership effectiveness with Sodapop compared to other retailers.

B. Regional Profitability Analysis

Regions were compared using Operating Margin (%).

* South region has the highest operating margin at 40.63%

Interpretation:
The South region demonstrates:

* Efficient cost management

* Strong pricing strategy

* Favorable demand-supply balance

This makes it the most profitable region, even if not always the highest in volume.

C. Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

Monthly aggregation of Total Sales reveals:

* December recorded the highest sales: $1,045,775

* July is the second-highest sales month

Business Explanation:

* December → Holiday season demand

* July → Summer beverage consumption peak

This confirms clear seasonality in sales patterns.

D. Pricing vs Volume Analysis

Comparison between:

* Price per Unit

* Units Sold across regions shows varying price sensitivity.

Observation:

* Some regions sustain high demand despite higher prices

* Other regions show volume drop with price increases

Insight:
Pricing sensitivity is region-specific, not uniform across markets, indicating an opportunity for localized pricing strategies.

5️⃣ Key Insights Summary

* Sodapop dominates revenue contribution among retailers

* South region delivers the highest profitability (40.63% margin)

* Sales peak in December and July, confirming seasonality

* Price sensitivity varies by region

* High revenue does not always imply high margin

6️⃣ Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

* Strengthen partnership and shelf presence with Sodapop

* Replicate South region’s margin strategy in other regions

* Increase inventory and promotions during December and July

* Implement region-specific pricing strategies

* Improve seasonal inventory planning to avoid stock-outs or overstocking
